Why, you ask?

Written by me

I needed to do something that had consequences. Do you ever think about that? Nothing really seems to have any. Whatever you do or say, nothing really ever changes.

Are you and I alive? Has life always been like this? Why do we have to know what void is? Emptyness. Everywhere.

We have to do something. Made up a meaning if there is none. We gotta do that, do you understand? We either find a reason to live, or perish. And don’t say that I’m exaggerating because I’m drunk. I drunk because I wanted to. Feel. Anything. And you know that.

Happy new year, my friend.
